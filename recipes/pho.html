Beef Pho

Description

Pho is simply a broth with flat rice noodles, a topping of your choice of proteins, and finally a dash of green onions and herbs.

Ingredients

  • bone broth
  • beef thinly sliced
  • rice noodles
  • fish sauce
  • roasted onions
  • ginger, star anise and salt
  • other condiments: basil, cilantro, jalapenos, etc

Steps

  1. Roast the beef bones and onions until the bones are browned and the onions are soft
  2. Make the broth
  3. Soak the rice noodles for an hour. Meanwhile, bring the broth to a simmer.
  4. Boil the noodles, then drain and add them to a bowl.
  5. Top with sirloin, cilantro, green onion, and broth. Garnish with your desired toppings.
