Menudo

Description

Menudo is a traditional Mexican soup made with cow tripe in a chilli pepper broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef tripe
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 15 ounce of white hominy
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a 16 quart pot combine tripe and onions
  2. Add water until pot is about 3/4 full
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for about 2 hours, or until the tripe is tender
  4. Add hominy, chili powder, and salt and pepper to taste
  5. Cover and cook for another 45 minutes to one hour to incorporate the flavors
  6. Serve with fresh onions, corn tortillas and lemon if desired
